About Chh. Shivajiraje Mahavidyalaya

Established in 1995, Chh. Shivajiraje Mahavidyalaya, Udgir, is a cornerstone of higher education in the region. The college offers undergraduate programs in Arts, Commerce, Science, and a Computer-based professional course, enrolling over 1600 students in its regular program. With a large campus, dedicated and highly qualified faculty (including 27 Ph.D. holders and 7 research guides), and a strong focus on research and community service (NSS, NCC, Earn and Learn scheme), the institution is committed to providing quality, holistic education for the advancement and betterment of the masses, as reflected by its NAAC A+ grade accreditation.

Our Values

  • Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of academic honesty and ethical conduct.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative thinking and adoption of new technologies.
  • Excellence: Striving for quality in teaching, research, and administration.
  • Inclusivity: Providing equal opportunities and a supportive environment for all students.

College History

Starting with a handful of traditional courses, the college grew rapidly, recognizing the need for technical education. The introduction of the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS) programs marked a significant milestone, aligning the institution with the demands of the modern workforce. We continue to adapt our curriculum to stay ahead of industry trends.

Governance and Administration

The college is governed by a dedicated board of management that ensures academic autonomy, financial stability, and adherence to regulatory guidelines. The Principal, supported by department heads and administrative staff, oversees the day-to-day operations.

Key Governing Bodies

  • Governing Body: Responsible for overall policy-making and strategic planning.
  • College Development Committee (CDC): Oversees development projects, financial management, and infrastructure planning.
  • Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC): Focuses on continuous improvement of academic and administrative performance.
  • Departmental Heads: Manage departmental affairs, curriculum implementation, and student performance.

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Program Overview

The BCA program is a professional course designed to produce skilled computer application specialists. It provides a strong foundation in modern programming languages, database management, web development, system management, and IT consulting.

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility

H.S.C. (10+2) or equivalent

Intake

60 Seats

Career Prospects

  • Software Developer/Engineer
  • Web Developer
  • Database Administrator
  • System Analyst
  • Network Administrator

Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)

Program Overview

The BCS program is an intensive three-year degree focusing on the theoretical foundations of computer science, mathematics, and programming. It is ideal for students aiming for advanced studies (like MCA/MSc) or research-oriented roles.

Duration

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility

H.S.C. (10+2) with PCM or equivalent

Intake

60 Seats

Career Prospects

  • System Programmer
  • Data Scientist/Analyst (Entry-level)
  • Further studies: MCA, MSc, MBA (IT)
  • Software Tester
  • Academic/Research Assistant

Admission Process (2024-25)

Follow these simple steps to secure your admission into the BCA or BCS program for the academic year 2024-25:

1

Online Pre-Registration

Fill out the mandatory online pre-registration form. Upload necessary preliminary documents (Mark sheets, ID Proof).

Go to Pre-Registration Portal
2

Document Verification & Counseling

Visit the college for physical document verification and a short counseling session with the HOD.

3

Fee Payment

Pay the required academic fees (can be done online or offline at the college counter).

4

Admission Confirmation

Collect your official admission receipt and student ID. You are now officially enrolled!

Academic Curricula & Subjects

BCA Curriculum Structure (CBCS)

  • Year I (Sem I & II):

    Introduction to Programming (C), Discrete Mathematics, Digital Electronics, Communication Skills, Operating System Fundamentals.

  • Year II (Sem III & IV):

    Data Structures, Object-Oriented Programming (C++), DBMS, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Linux Administration.

  • Year III (Sem V & VI):

    Web Technology (PHP/Python), Java Programming, Data Science Fundamentals, Cloud Computing, Project Work & Internships.

BCS Curriculum Structure (CBCS)

  • Year I (Sem I & II):

    Foundational Mathematics, Digital Logic, Programming in C, Statistical Methods, Core Physics/Electronics.

  • Year II (Sem III & IV):

    Advanced Data Structures, OOP with Java, Database Systems, Computer Architecture, Advanced Calculus & Algebra.

  • Year III (Sem V & VI):

    Computer Graphics, Operating System Internals, Web Programming, Data Communication, Elective Stream, Research Project.
